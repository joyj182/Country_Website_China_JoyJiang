The Great Wall of China

Location: Northern China

The Great Wall is one of the most famous structures in the world. It was built to protect ancient China from invasions and stretches over 13,000 miles.

The Forbidden City

Location: Beijing

The Forbidden City is a massive palace complex that served as the home of Chinese emperors for over 500 years.

The Terracotta Army

Location: Xi'an

The Terracotta Army consists of thousands of life-sized soldiers buried to protect China's first emperor in the afterlife.
